Site Purpose

Help users memorize scripture verses from sacred writings such as the Book of Mormon and the Bible.

Scenarios

How can I contact the developers of this webpage?

The contact us page provides way to communicate with the developers of this website.

How can I find a specific verse?

Search bar and filter buttons are provided in the page where the list of scriptures are displayed.

Why do I need this site?

The home page provides information about the importance and benefits of this site.

Can I use this site for my kids and my students?

Yes! As long as it's not for commercial purposes, you are free to do so.
